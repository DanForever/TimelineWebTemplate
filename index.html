Catchy subtitle with lots of synergy

A digital, readable, and customizable watchface for your Pebble Time.
Inspired by the visual language of the Timeline, TimeStyle shows only the information most important to you, including current weather, today's forecast, the current date, the week number, the time in another time zone, the battery level, or even seconds!
  • Match your style: select from over 20 preset color schemes, or mix and match colors create, save, and share your own custom themes!
  • Focus on what you need: three selectable complications on the sidebar allow you to choose the information is important to you
  • Know when you've walked away from your phone: displays a bluetooth disconnection notification, with an optional vibration
  • Works in your language: With 22 languages, TimeStyle supports more languages than any other watchface. Choose between English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Dutch, Turkish, Czech, Slovak, Portuguese, Greek, Swedish, Polish, Romanian, Vietnamese, Catalan, Norwegian, Russian, Estonian, Basque, Finnish, and Danish!
